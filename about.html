About Application

The application which we have developed is website for online comparison.It is often observed that customers waste a lot of their precious time by browsing through innumerable stores to find the best product and price. But our website help all such customers.We have designed the website in such a way that it will contain the results of both flipkart and ebay. When users search anything in our site then the prices available in both flipkart and ebay are displayed.When they click on the particular item or price which they like then they will be directed to the corresponding website where they can easily purchase the item.
